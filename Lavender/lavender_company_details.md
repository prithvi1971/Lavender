# Lavender Psychiatry

Lavender Psychiatry is an online platform that provides talk therapy and medication management services. 

## Key Offerings:
- Talk therapy
- Medication management

## Conditions Treated:
They offer care for a range of mental health conditions including:
- Anxiety
- Depression
- ADHD
- Bipolar disorder
- Trauma
- Insomnia
- OCD
- Life transitions

## Insurance & Availability:
- The service is in-network with most major insurance providers.
- Operates in numerous US states.

## Additional Resources:
- The website includes client testimonials and a blog with mental health advice.
