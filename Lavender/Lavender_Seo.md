# Lavender SEO Strategy Document

This document outlines key SEO (Search Engine Optimization) keywords currently used within Lavender's content, primarily derived from blog post analysis, and suggests new keywords to enhance online visibility and reach.

## 1. Current SEO Used (Based on Blog Content Analysis)

The following keywords and phrases are consistently used across Lavender's blog posts, reflecting the primary mental health concerns and services they address. These terms are found in titles, topics, and the body content of the articles.

### Mental Health Conditions/Issues:
*   Anxiety
*   Depression
*   ADHD (Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder)
*   Postpartum Depression (PPD)
*   Perinatal Care (related to PPD)
*   Grief / Loss
*   Emotional Burnout
*   Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD) / Winter Blues
*   Chronic Illness (mental health aspects)
*   Infertility (mental health aspects)
*   Trauma
*   Bipolar Disorder (less frequent, but present)
*   OCD (Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder, less frequent, but present)

### Therapies/Services/Concepts:
*   Therapy / Talk Therapy
*   Medication Management / Psychiatric Medication / SSRIs
*   Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner (Psych NP / PMHNP)
*   Self-Care
*   Stress Management / Coping Strategies
*   Mindfulness
*   Boundaries / Healthy Boundaries
*   Goal Setting
*   Sleep Habits / Insomnia
*   Emotional Wellness / Mental Well-being
*   Life Transitions (e.g., Retirement, Parenting, New Friendships, Holidays)
*   Nutrition / Food and Mental Health
*   Outdoor / Nature Benefits (for mental health)

### Demographics/Specific Situations:
*   Parents / Parenting / Motherhood / Dads
*   Students
*   Holidays / Holiday Season
*   Election Stress
*   Pandemic Fatigue / Being Home (historically relevant)

### Branding/Service Specific:
*   Lavender / Lavender Psychiatry
*   Online therapy / Telehealth (implied by service, sometimes explicit in CTAs)
*   Virtual mental health
